Self-development is the shit!

We want to make it easier for everyone interested in organizaitonal development and organizational psychology to stay up to date on the newest, relevant research within the field. Based on publications and research by some of the most recognized researchers, we summarize their articles and research through summaries and infographics.

The summer of 2020 we both finished our Master of Science in Leadership and Organizational Psychology at BI Norwegian Business School. We soon found out that we both were interested in organizational development and organizational psychology in general. Both on the individual- and organizational level. We hope that we could help others with the same interest as us to increase their knowledge and self-develop.

By students, for students. This page is a tool for individuals seeking to self-develop by increasing their knowledge. By summarizing relevant and up-to-date research in text-form and through infographics we make this easy for you!
